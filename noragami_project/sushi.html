Sushi

    1. Prepare Rice: Cook sushi rice and mix with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.

    2. Slice Fillings: Cut your choice of fillings (like fish, cucumber, or avocado) into thin strips.

    3. Lay Nori: Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat.

    4. Spread Rice: Evenly spread a layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving a small border.

    5. Roll & Slice: Add fillings, roll tightly, and slice into pieces. Enjoy!
