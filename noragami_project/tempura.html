Tempura

    1. Prepare Batter: Mix cold water, flour, and a pinch of salt until just combined.

    2. Heat Oil: Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan to 350°F (175°C).

    3. Coat Ingredients: Dip vegetables or shrimp into the batter, allowing excess to drip off.

    4. Fry: Fry in hot oil until golden and crispy, about 2-3 minutes.

    5. Drain & Serve: Remove, drain on paper towels, and serve with dipping sauce. Enjoy!
