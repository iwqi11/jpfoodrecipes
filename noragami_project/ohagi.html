Ohagi

    1. Cook Rice: Rinse and soak glutinous rice for a few hours, then steam or boil until tender.

    2. Mash Rice: Once cooked, mash the rice while it's warm until smooth.

    3. Shape: Take a small portion of the rice and flatten it in your hand. Place a spoonful of red bean paste in the center, then wrap the rice around it to form a ball.

    4. Dust: Roll the finished ohagi in cornstarch to prevent sticking.

    5. Serve: Enjoy as a sweet treat!
